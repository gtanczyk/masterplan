HOWTO multiplayer

It's simple, and you can do it while being offline!

  1. Copy your blueprint to clipboard
  2. Send to a friend, or tweet to #masterplan_js13k
  3. Receive blueprint from other player
  4. Paste (near "Play" button)
  5. Play the battle
  6. Improve your blueprint
  7. Repeat!
